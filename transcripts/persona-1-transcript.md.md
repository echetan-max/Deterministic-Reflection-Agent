# DeepThought Daily Reflection — Sample Transcripts

**Tree file:** `reflection-tree-final.tsv`
**Purpose:** Two complete walkthroughs demonstrating how the same tree branches differently based on the employee's answers, producing distinct reflections and summaries.

---

## How to read these transcripts

Each node is labelled with its ID in brackets. The tree has three axes in sequence:

| Axis | Theme | Spectrum |
|---|---|---|
| Axis 1 | Locus of Control | External (victim) ↔ Internal (victor) |
| Axis 2 | Orientation | Entitlement ↔ Contribution |
| Axis 3 | Radius of Concern | Self-centric ↔ Altrocentric |

Decision nodes are silent — they branch automatically based on the previous answer and produce no visible text. They are noted in the branch map but not shown in the conversation.

# Transcript 1 — Persona: Maya
### "Victim · Entitled · Self-Centric"

> **Character sketch:** Maya is a mid-level analyst. Today was heavy: a project deadline was moved up without warning, a colleague got credit she felt was hers, and she spent most of the afternoon waiting for her manager to unblock her. She arrives at the reflection tool tired, a little resentful, and not yet ready to see her own role in the day.

---

**[START]**

🌿 *Good evening. Take a breath. This is not a performance review; it is a quiet look at how you showed up today.*

---

**[A1_OPEN — Question]**

🌿 How would you describe today in one word?

> A) Productive
> B) Challenging
> C) Mixed
> **D) Draining**

**Maya picks: Draining**

---

*[A1_D1 — Decision: "Draining" → routes to A1_Q_AGENCY_LOW]*

---

**[A1_Q_AGENCY_LOW — Question]**

🌿 You said *"Draining"*. When things got hard today, what was your first move?

> A) I looked for what I could control
> B) I pushed through alone
> **C) I waited for things to settle**
> D) I felt blocked and stuck

**Maya picks: I waited for things to settle**

---

*[A1_D2_LOW — Decision: "I waited for things to settle" → routes to A1_Q_CHOICE_EXTERNAL]*

---

**[A1_Q_CHOICE_EXTERNAL — Question]**

🌿 Think of one frustrating moment. Looking back, was there a small move you could have made?

> A) Yes, I see one now
> B) Maybe, with more support
> **C) Probably not, it was out of my hands**
> D) I am not sure yet

**Maya picks: Probably not, it was out of my hands**

---

*[A1_D3_EXTERNAL — Decision: "Probably not…" → routes to A1_R_EXTERNAL]*
*Signal recorded: axis1:external*

---

**[A1_R_EXTERNAL — Reflection]**

✦ *Today felt like something that happened to you. That is worth noticing without blame. The useful question is: where was even one small choice?*

---

**[BRIDGE_1_2_C — Bridge]**

*After a difficult day, it is natural to notice what went wrong. Now let us look at what you gave, even in a small way.*

---

**[A2_OPEN — Question]**

🌿 Think about one interaction you had today. What was your main focus in that moment?

> A) Making sure they had what they needed
> **B) Getting what I needed from them**
> C) Being heard and understood
> D) Getting through it quickly

**Maya picks: Getting what I needed from them**

---

*[A2_D1 — Decision: "Getting what I needed from them" → routes to A2_Q_ENTITLEMENT]*

---

**[A2_Q_ENTITLEMENT — Question]**

🌿 When you needed support, recognition, or a response today and did not get it, what went through your mind?

> A) I noticed the gap but moved on
> B) I wondered what I could do differently
> C) It felt unfair given my effort
> **D) I started thinking about who owed what**

**Maya picks: I started thinking about who owed what**

---

*[A2_D2_ENTITLEMENT — Decision: "I started thinking about who owed what" → routes to A2_Q_EXTRA_ENTITLEMENT]*
*Signal recorded: axis2:entitlement*

---

**[A2_Q_EXTRA_ENTITLEMENT — Question]**

🌿 When you compare what you gave today with what you received, how does it feel?

> A) Balanced enough
> **B) I gave more than I got**
> C) I got what I needed
> D) I am not sure I contributed as much as I could have

**Maya picks: I gave more than I got**

---

*[A2_D3_ENTITLEMENT — Decision: "I gave more than I got" → routes to A2_R_ENTITLEMENT]*
*Signal recorded: axis2:entitlement*

---

**[A2_R_ENTITLEMENT — Reflection]**

✦ *A quiet ledger showed up today: what you gave versus what you got back. That is not a failure, but it is a signal to notice before it becomes your default lens.*

---

**[BRIDGE_2_3_C — Bridge]**

*You noticed what you wanted from the day. Now let us ask how much of your awareness extended beyond yourself.*

---

**[A3_OPEN — Question]**

🌿 When you think about today's hardest moment, who came to mind first?

> **A) Just me**
> B) My immediate team
> C) One person who had it harder
> D) The customer or end user

**Maya picks: Just me**

---

*[A3_D1 — Decision: "Just me" → routes to A3_Q_SELF]*

---

**[A3_Q_SELF — Question]**

🌿 When you were focused on yourself today, what were you mainly trying to protect?

> A) My energy
> B) My time
> **C) My reputation**
> D) My peace of mind

**Maya picks: My reputation**

---

*[A3_D2_SELF — Decision: "My reputation" → routes to A3_Q_BRIDGE_CHECK]*
*Signal recorded: axis3:self*

---

**[A3_Q_BRIDGE_CHECK — Question]**

🌿 Looking back, how much of your energy went outward versus inward?

> A) Mostly outward
> B) Balanced
> **C) Mostly inward**
> D) Hard to say

**Maya picks: Mostly inward**

---

*[A3_D3_BRIDGE — Decision: "Mostly inward" → routes to A3_R_SELF]*
*Signal recorded: axis3:mixed*

---

**[A3_R_SELF — Reflection]**

✦ *Today your attention stayed mostly inside your own experience. That is understandable on heavy days. Tomorrow, one small outward question could change the shape of the day.*

---

**[SUMMARY — Summary]**

🌿 *Today you leaned **external** on agency, **entitlement** on contribution, and **self** in your radius of concern. This is not a score. It is a mirror: one clear way to notice how you showed up and one small place to grow tomorrow.*

---

**[END]**

🌿 *See you tomorrow. You showed up today, and that counts.*
